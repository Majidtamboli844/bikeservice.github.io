Our Services

General Service

Comprehensive check-up, oil change, chain lubrication, brake and clutch adjustment, and general tuning for optimal performance.

  • Engine oil replacement
  • Brake and clutch adjustment
  • Chain cleaning & lubrication
  • Spark plug cleaning

Puncture Repair

Quick and efficient puncture repair for tubeless and tube tires, ensuring you're back on the road in no time.

  • Tubeless tire repair
  • Tube tire replacement/repair
  • Tire pressure check

Bike Washing & Detailing

Thorough cleaning, polishing, and detailing to make your bike look brand new. Includes waxing and chrome cleaning.

  • Foam wash
  • Waxing & polishing
  • Chrome cleaning
  • Interior detailing

Brake System Check

Inspection and adjustment of brake pads, fluid levels, and disc/drum condition for optimal braking safety.

  • Brake pad inspection
  • Brake fluid top-up/replacement
  • Disc/drum cleaning

Battery Check & Replacement

Testing battery health, cleaning terminals, and replacement if necessary with high-quality batteries.

  • Battery health test
  • Terminal cleaning
  • Battery replacement (if needed)

Custom Repairs

We offer custom repairs and part replacements based on your bike's specific needs and issues.

  • Part replacement
  • Engine diagnostics
  • Electrical system repair

About Us

At BikeCare Pro, we are passionate about bikes and committed to providing unparalleled doorstep service. Founded with the vision of making bike maintenance hassle-free, we bring expert mechanics and genuine parts directly to your location.

Our team of certified professionals is equipped to handle everything from routine maintenance to complex repairs, ensuring your bike runs smoothly and safely. We pride ourselves on transparency, efficiency, and customer satisfaction. Experience the convenience of premium bike service without leaving your home or office!
